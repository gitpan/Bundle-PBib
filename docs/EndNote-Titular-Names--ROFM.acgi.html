How do I enter Titular names (e.g., Leo XIII, Vatican Council II, Elizabeth II) so the author appears as "John Paul II," "Leo XIII," or Elizabeth II in the citation or reference list? How do I enter company names as authors so that the entire company name appears in the citation or reference list?

Record Entered: 7/27/2001, Record #418

Response:

Both Mac & PC:
Enter the names into the database as last names only, with a comma following the entire name, such as:

Leo XIII,
Vatican Council II,
John Paul II,
Elizabeth II,
